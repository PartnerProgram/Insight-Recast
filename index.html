The Case in Brief

  • Third-party application management is the top customer demand inside Managed Endpoint 2.0 and the slowest-scaling work today.
  • Packaging and delivery are duplicated across SCCM, Intune, Citrix, and AVD; capacity scales with platforms, not headcount.
  • Application Workspace was validated end-to-end: managed apps, custom packaging, user experience, dashboarding, multi-tenancy.
  • Premier provides the deepest economics, partner-of-record protection, and a direct line to Recast leadership.

Methodology & Assumptions

All outputs are deterministic functions of the inputs shown in the Business Case Model. No figure is hard-coded; every result recalculates from the assumptions.

Calculation Basis

  • Licensing GP = (Insight price − Recast rate) × seats × 12.
  • Services GP = attach× × licensing revenue × services margin.
  • Implementation GP = projects × fee × margin.
  • P&L Gross Profit = licensing GP + services GP + implementation GP (cash margin).
  • Efficiency value = reclaimed FTE × loaded engineer cost; reported separately as avoided cost, not in P&L.
  • Reclaimed FTE = manual app-lifecycle FTE − FTE with AW, where AW only packages custom apps at the efficiency multiple.
  • Recast rate steps by volume tier: $1.00 (≤100K), $0.90 (≤250K), $0.80 (≤500K).

Key Assumptions & Sources

  • Premier terms: $600K Year-1 minimum, $1.00 entry rate, 3-year rate lock, volume tiers as above.
  • Efficiency multiple (~7×) reflects documented packaging time reduction (~8 hrs → ~2 hrs; ~1/8th time observed by partners).
  • Manual application work ~60% of effort, targeted to ~20% post-deployment.
  • Services attach (default 4×, conservative) is a planning assumption — the primary item to validate.
  • Recast: 8,000+ pre-tested apps; 60M+ endpoints managed; SOC 2 Type II / ISO 27001.
  • Figures are planning estimates for internal evaluation, not guarantees.
